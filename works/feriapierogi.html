Feria del Pierogi

Launch Campaign | Final Degree Project

Launch graphic campaign for the first edition of the Pierogi Fest in Valencia (fictitious project).

This gastronomic event that revolves around Poland's most typical dish, the pierogi, is a joyful occasion to enjoy this delicacy and get to know new cultures.

The campaign is aimed at both the Polish population residing in Valencia and the rest of the Valencian population. People who like to spend time with family and are interested in the gastronomic world.

We wanted to give this campaign a folklorical, familiar and joyful tone. Because it is a Polish cuisine fair, it is closely related to the culture of the country, its traditions and history, that's why the concept of folklore had to be present. Another concept is joy, since we want to convey a message or image that shows the festive and summer atmosphere of the fair. Finally, we chose the family concept because gastronomy is something that we usually share and enjoy with those closest to us, and we want to convey that it will be an event for everyone.

We designed a campaign in which the atmosphere of the fair and the pierogi are the protagonists. Through synthetic and close illustrations, we create compositions in which we show different aspects of the fair such as the Polish culture, the food it offers and others. We also design a dynamic and irregular typography, with shapes that adapt to each other and create a cheerful and fun design. We use this typography as the corporate logo of the fair, and it is present in all the elements of the campaign.

This campaign has been developed using the design tools Adobe Fresco, Adobe Illustrator and Figma. Since the main resource of our design was vectorial illustration, we have used programs designed to work with this type of illustration.
